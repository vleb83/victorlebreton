PropTech Startup · From Scratch · Investment Memo

RentWise — Investment Memo & Financial Structuring

Context: Brought in as Financial Specialist on a PropTech startup building a property management platform for individual landlords in the Paris region. The mandate was to build the financial foundation of the company from the ground up — not just model numbers, but craft an investment-ready narrative for early-stage fundraising.

Problem: The founding team had a strong operational vision but no structured financial framework — no market sizing, no revenue model formalisation, no projections, and no investor-facing financial story.

Methodology:

  • Bottom-up market sizing: TAM (€1B+), SAM (€311M), SOM (€31M) for the IDF rental market
  • Revenue model structuring: 4.2% management fee + agency fees + premium subscription tiers
  • Financial projections & breakeven analysis from pre-revenue to scale
  • KPI dashboard design: occupancy rates, payment delay metrics, LTV/CAC
  • Competitive landscape mapping vs. Flatlooker, Liberkeys, Blue
  • Full pitch deck financial narrative for seed fundraising

Deliverables: Complete investor pitch deck (32 slides), financial model, market sizing memo, and KPI framework.
Impact: Structured a compelling, investor-ready financial story for a zero-to-one PropTech venture targeting a €31M serviceable market.
