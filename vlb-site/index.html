About

Victor Le Breton

I am a Fund Management Analyst at one of Europe's leading real estate asset managers, with 5+ years of experience across fund modelling, acquisition and financing structuring, and investor reporting. I regularly support investment committees on transactions and portfolio decisions exceeding €1bn in assets under management.

My background is rooted in institutional real estate finance, where precision, discipline, and speed of execution are non-negotiable. I've worked on the full investment lifecycle — from underwriting and structuring to performance monitoring and investor communication — across complex, multi-asset portfolios.

Alongside this, I advise startups, scale-ups and SMEs on building robust finance functions. This includes financial modelling from scratch, FP&A setup, budgeting cycles, and decision-support tools tailored to fast-moving environments.

I operate as a hands-on finance partner, combining institutional-grade analytical standards with the flexibility and pragmatism required by growing businesses.
